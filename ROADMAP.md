# GreenPulse Development Roadmap

## 1. File Upload & AI Analysis

**Feature:** Document upload in AI assistant

- Add file upload button in frontend
- Store uploaded files in Supabase Storage (signed URLs)
- Extract text content from PDFs/documents
- Pass extracted text to AI for environmental report analysis
- Store analysis results in database

---

## 2. AI Service Evolution

**Transform AI from chatbot → Environmental Intelligence & Compliance System**

### New AI Capabilities:

- **Industry Report Analysis** - Accept operational data and environmental reports
- **What-If Scenarios** - Answer predictive questions about planned actions
- **Environmental Impact Prediction** - Forecast consequences of business decisions
- **Regulatory Compliance** - Check against environmental regulations
- **Mitigation Strategies** - Suggest alternative energy options and risk reduction
- **Future Conditions Analysis** - Help businesses understand area-specific climate trends

**Goal:** Decision-support system for climate-smart, compliant operations

---

## 3. Telegram Bot Enhancement

**Make bot more conversational and intelligent**

- Natural human-AI dialogue (not just commands)
- Answer diverse questions: weather forecasts, climate trends, conservation tips
- Provide location-based environmental insights
- Proactive suggestions based on user region

---

## 4. Cron Job Update

**Change from risk detection → Automated messaging**

- Send daily/weekly environmental updates to subscribed users
- Deliver climate forecasts and alerts via Telegram
- Automated report summaries
- Scheduled compliance reminders

---

## Implementation Priority

1. AI Service redesign (core functionality)
2. Telegram bot conversational upgrade
3. File upload system
4. Cron messaging automation
